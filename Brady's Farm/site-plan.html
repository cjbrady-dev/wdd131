Overview

Purpose

This Website is a trial website I am making for my Father. He needs a website he can use to advertise his farm on and what animals are for sale. This project should be a basic advertisement website and allow customers to contact my father and send him a small email with their information.

Audience

The audience of this project will be focused of ranchers or farmers that need to buy animals such as cows or cow dogs even horses.

Dynamic elements

The Javascript should be able to allow customers to contact my father and send him a small email with their information.
