Overview

Purpose

This Website is a trial website I am making for my Father's animal farm business. He needs a website he can use to advertise his farm on and what animals are for sale to those looking to buy them. This project should be a basic advertisement website and allow customers to see what is for sell to contact my father and send him a small email with their information if they are interested.

Audience

The audience of this project will be focused of ranchers or farmers that need to buy animals such as cows or cow dogs even horses.

Dynamic elements

The Javascript should be able to allow more than one item to be entered in the Javascript and then populate as a list of items showing on page 2 of the site, with a picture and information of the animal.
